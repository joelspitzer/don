Donald W. Zeigler, PhD

Public Health Advocate, Educator, and Prevention Specialist

Professional Profile

Dr. Donald Zeigler is a nationally recognized public health leader with over five decades of experience in health promotion. He is a specialist in the intersection of global trade policy and non-communicable disease prevention, with a particular focus on alcohol and tobacco control.

"Dedicated to protecting local health outcomes by navigating the complexities of international trade and national health-care reform."
